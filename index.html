Cytology

How To Play

To play, you answer questions to gain ATP (1 question = 2 ATP). Use your ATP to defend your cell from pathogens that try to tear your cell's membrane by tugging at it! Use your ATP to slice the pathogens in half to eliminate them!


You must protect your cell while your answer only 25 questions. If your cell's membrane gets torn, you lose!

To slice a pathogen, you have to click and drag over it.
